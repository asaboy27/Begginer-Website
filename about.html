About Us

🇦🇺TipToe Soles is an Australian owned and operated company. We aim to provide comfortable footwear at affordable prices to customers within Australia and around the world. We have been operating since 1975 and have been helping the Australian people by donating a percentage of our profit each time we sell one of our shoes.

We here at TipToe Soles, are an advocate for protecting yourself from the harsh Australian sun. The shoes we offers are at an atmost highest quality within the same price range, considering the price of our shoes are very reasonable.

You'll never pay full price for a pair of strappy platform pumps, ankle boots, or everyday flats again since our team of committed sneaker fanatics, high heel heroes, & sandal stylists added every deal they could locate! We continually add new items to our collection, including heels, ballerina flats, boots, sneakers, and sandals. Our selection of top-tier global comfort shoe manufacturers.
